# Wiki or Whack? 🎉
**Wikipedia 25th Anniversary Trivia — Wiki Club SATI, July 18 2026**

Real-time multiplayer trivia. Up to 40+ players on their phones, one big screen host.

---

## On event day — 3 steps

**1. Big screen (you)**
Open this link on the laptop connected to the projector, then press F11:
`https://manasmishra8967-bit.github.io/Wiki-or-Whack/host.html`

**2. Players**
They open this on their phones (or scan a QR code):
`https://manasmishra8967-bit.github.io/Wiki-or-Whack/`

**3. Start**
Watch the player count go up → hit **Start Game** when ready → click **Next Question** after each reveal.

---

## Scoring
- Up to **10 points** per question
- Answer in 1s = 10 pts · Answer in 15s = ~2 pts · No answer = 0 pts
- 3 of every 5 questions = real Wikipedia facts · 2 of 5 = AI-generated

## Reset between rounds
Click **Reset Game** on the host screen. Clears all players and scores.
